   112|- **Market Segmentation**: Demographic, psychographic, behavioral, geographic with personas
   113|- **Growth Projections**: Historical trends, driver analysis, scenario modeling with risk factors

   118|- **Usage Patterns**: Frequency, context, satisfaction with behavioral clustering
