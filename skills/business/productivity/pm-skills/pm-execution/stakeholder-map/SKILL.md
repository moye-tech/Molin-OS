    15|## Stakeholder Mapping & Communication Plan
    16|
    17|Map stakeholders on a Power × Interest grid and create a tailored communication plan for each group.
    18|
    19|### Context
    20|
    21|You are helping build a stakeholder map for **$ARGUMENTS**.
    22|
    23|If the user provides files (org charts, project briefs, team rosters), read them first. If they describe the product or initiative, use that context to infer likely stakeholders.
    24|
    25|### Instructions
    26|
    27|1. **Identify stakeholders**: List all relevant individuals and groups — executives, engineering leads, designers, marketing, sales, support, legal, finance, external partners, and end users.
    28|
    29|2. **Classify each stakeholder** on two dimensions:
    30|   - **Power** (High/Low): Their ability to influence decisions, resources, or outcomes
    31|   - **Interest** (High/Low): How much the project directly affects them or how engaged they are
    32|
    33|3. **Place stakeholders in the Power × Interest grid**:
    34|
    35|   | | High Interest | Low Interest |
    36|   |---|---|---|
    37|   | **High Power** | **Manage Closely** — Regular 1:1s, involve in decisions, seek their input early | **Keep Satisfied** — Periodic updates, escalate only critical issues |
    38|   | **Low Power** | **Keep Informed** — Regular status updates, invite to demos, gather feedback | **Monitor** — Light-touch updates, available on request |
    39|
    40|4. **For each quadrant**, recommend:
    41|   - Communication frequency (daily, weekly, bi-weekly, monthly)
    42|   - Communication format (1:1, email, Slack, meeting, dashboard)
    43|   - Key messages and framing
    44|   - Potential risks if this stakeholder is neglected
    45|
    46|5. **Create a communication plan table**:
    47|
    48|   | Stakeholder | Role | Power | Interest | Strategy | Frequency | Channel | Key Message |
    49|   |---|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
    50|
    51|6. **Flag potential conflicts**: Identify stakeholders with competing interests and suggest alignment strategies.
    52|
    53|Think step by step. Save the stakeholder map as a markdown document.
