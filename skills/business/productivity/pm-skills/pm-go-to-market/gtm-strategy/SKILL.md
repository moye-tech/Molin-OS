    14|# GTM Strategy
    15|
    16|## Overview
    17|Create a comprehensive go-to-market strategy for a product launch. This skill covers marketing channels, messaging development, success metrics definition, and launch planning.
    18|
    19|## When to Use
    20|- Planning a product launch
    21|- Creating a GTM plan from scratch
    22|- Defining a launch strategy for a new market
    23|- Developing product-to-market fit strategy
    24|- Preparing a product go-live roadmap
    25|
    26|## How It Works
    27|
    28|### Step 1: Gather Research Data
    29|The system will help you load and analyze early research about your product and target market. Provide:
    30|- Product description and key features
    31|- Target market segment details
    32|- Market research or validation data
    33|- Competitive landscape information
    34|- Any available customer interviews or survey data
    35|
    36|### Step 2: Define Marketing Channels
    37|Evaluate which channels best reach your target audience:
    38|- Digital marketing channels (paid search, social media, display)
    39|- Content and inbound channels (blog, SEO, thought leadership)
    40|- Sales and outbound channels (direct outreach, partnerships)
    41|- Community and grassroots channels
    42|- Product-led and viral channels
    43|
    44|### Step 3: Develop Messaging
    45|Create audience-specific messaging that resonates:
    46|- Core value proposition for target segment
    47|- Key differentiators and competitive advantages
    48|- Pain point validation and solution mapping
    49|- Proof points and social proof strategies
    50|- Channel-specific messaging variations
    51|
    52|### Step 4: Define Success Metrics
    53|Establish measurable KPIs to track launch success:
    54|- Awareness metrics (impressions, reach, brand recall)
    55|- Engagement metrics (CTR, cost per engagement, time on site)
    56|- Conversion metrics (signups, demos requested, trials started)
    57|- Revenue metrics (MRR, customer acquisition cost, lifetime value)
    58|- Market metrics (market share, segment penetration)
    59|
    60|### Step 5: Create Launch Plan
    61|Build a phased launch timeline:
    62|- Pre-launch preparation (messaging, channels, timeline)
    63|- Launch day activities and announcements
    64|- Post-launch momentum (content, partnerships, communities)
    65|- Measurement and optimization cadence
    66|- Success criteria and go/no-go decision points
    67|
    68|## Input Format
    69|Use $ARGUMENTS to pass:
    70|- Product name and description
    71|- Target market segment
    72|- Research data or file path
    73|- Launch timeline and constraints
    74|- Budget or resource limitations
    75|
    76|## Output
    77|A structured GTM strategy document including:
    78|- Recommended marketing channels with justification
    79|- Channel-specific messaging and positioning
    80|- Launch timeline with key milestones
    81|- KPI targets and measurement framework
    82|- Risk mitigation strategies
    83|- 90-day execution roadmap
    84|
    85|## Framework
    86|This skill applies Product Compass GTM strategy methodology, focusing on market selection, channel fit, and message-market fit for sustainable product growth.
    87|
    88|## Tips
    89|- Start with your most confident customer segment
    90|- Validate assumptions through customer interviews before full launch
    91|- Focus on a few channels excellently rather than many channels poorly
    92|- Establish baseline metrics before launch to measure impact
    93|- Plan for feedback loops and optimization
